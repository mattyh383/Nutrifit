Welcome

The Nutrifit philosophy is: 'Optimum nutrition is the cornerstone to good health'.

However with increased demands on our time consuming and stressful lifestyles, this can often lead to erratic and unhealthy eating patterns. Many people often skip meals or grab something on the go. An increasing number of people rely heavily on convenience foods, over-processed foods and high fat sugary snacks as the foundation of their diet. Nutrifit believe the link between food and exercise is the key to a happy healthy body and successful weight loss. Commercial weight loss programmes are impersonal, and for many, impractical. Our approach is personal and individual. It looks at you lifestyle, current fitness level and any medical concerns; rather than addressing diet alone.

About Me

I have worked in the fitness and nutrition industry for over fifteen years and have made a difference to so many lives through my healthy living, functional medicine and weight loss programmes. I founded Nutrifit in 2002 and currently work from my practice in Rowstock, Oxfordshire. I see clients on a one-to-one for nutritional therapy or in groups for weight loss programmes.
